Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about OfferMarket

🤔 When will OfferMarket be available?

We're planning to launch our beta version in Q1 2024, with the full public release scheduled for Q3 2024. Sign up for early access to be among the first to try it!

💰 Will OfferMarket be free to use?

Yes! OfferMarket will be completely free to download and use. We may offer optional premium features in the future for power users.

📱 Which platforms will be supported?

OfferMarket will be available on iOS and Android devices at launch. We're also planning a web version for desktop users.

🏪 Which stores will be included?

We're actively partnering with major grocery chains, department stores, pharmacies, and local retailers. Our goal is to include all major retailers by launch.

🔒 How will you protect my data?

Privacy is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data and never sell your personal information to third parties.

🚀 How can I become a beta tester?

Sign up for early access above! We'll select beta testers from our early access list and invite them to test the app before public launch.
